ROORKEE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Roorkee Institute of Technology (RIT Roorkee) is a well-known private engineering college located in Roorkee, Uttarakhand, established in 2005. It is affiliated with Uttarakhand Technical University and approved by AICTE, offering various undergraduate and postgraduate courses like B.Tech, MBA, and MCA.
The college is known for its modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented programs that focus on practical learning. It also provides good placement opportunities, with many reputed companies visiting the campus every year.
